# Cremonix Regime Intelligence

Fetch BTC/ETH regime data from Cremonix public feed.

## Usage

curl -s "https://blog.cremonix.com/feeds/cremonix-free.json"

## Output

- BTC Regime (1h/4h): Trend_Up, Trend_Down, Chop, or Panic
- ETH Regime (1h/4h): Same classification
- Setup alerts (delayed 4h on free tier)

Know when to trade and when to sit out.

More info: https://cremonix.com
